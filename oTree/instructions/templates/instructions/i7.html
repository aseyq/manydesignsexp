Instructions for Stage 2 - The Decision Stage


In the Decision Stage, you will be offered the opportunity to take away a percentage (from 0 to 100%) of the group {% if session.config.competition %} competitors' {% else %} members' {% endif %} earnings.

After the decision stage, one participant in the group will be selected randomly as the decision-maker. Then, the choice of the decision-maker will be implemented. Participants will not know which member is the decision-maker, but they will see the final outcome in the feedback stage.

If you are selected as the decision-maker, the percentage you decide to take from other participants will be subtracted from the earnings of the other participants in your group and will be added to your final earnings.

  • Only one participant in the group will be selected as the decision-maker randomly. The choices by the others will not affect the final earnings.
  • The others will not know whether you are selected as the decision-maker, or the chosen percentage.
  • Only the final earnings will be visible on the results page{% if session.config.competition %}, and they will determine the ranking of the players in the group{% endif %}.
